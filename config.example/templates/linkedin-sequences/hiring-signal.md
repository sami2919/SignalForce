# Hiring Signal LinkedIn Sequence

## Signal Context

An open RL engineer role signals that a team is scaling — they have scope that exceeds their current headcount. The hiring manager or technical lead is simultaneously dealing with the infrastructure burden that made the hire necessary. LinkedIn is effective here because the signal is public and the timing is right: they are actively thinking about capacity and tooling. Frame the connection around the RL domain, not the job posting.

## Placeholders

- `{{contact_first_name}}` — Recipient's first name
- `{{company_name}}` — Company name
- `{{signal_reference}}` — Job title from the posting (e.g., "Senior RL Engineer")
- `{{rl_use_case}}` — What the role suggests the company is building (inferred from JD)
- `{{rl_challenge}}` — The scaling or infrastructure challenge implied by the hire

---

## Connection Request Note (≤300 chars)

### Variant A: Signal Reference

Noticed {{company_name}} is hiring for {{signal_reference}} — suggests you're scaling {{rl_use_case}} seriously. Building in the same space and would value connecting with someone navigating that growth.

### Variant B: Mutual Interest

Working on RL infrastructure and following teams pushing {{rl_use_case}} into production. {{company_name}}'s work in this area looks genuinely interesting — would be glad to connect.
