# LinkedIn Content

## Why This Matters for Outbound

Cold outreach converts better when prospects recognize your name. Organic LinkedIn posts build credibility so that when your email arrives, you're a familiar voice — not a stranger. Use this skill to create posts that your ICP will see before they get your first email.

## Before Writing

Read `config/gtm-context.md` for brand voice, target personas, and domain terminology. Posts should sound like you, not like marketing copy.

## Post Anatomy

```
HOOK (first 1-2 lines)         ← Visible before "...see more"
...see more ──────────────────  ← The click gate (210 chars)
BODY (story/value)              ← Short paragraphs, 1-2 sentences each
CTA (last 1-2 lines)           ← Invite genuine discussion
#hashtags (3-5)
```

## Hook Formulas

| Type | Example |
|------|---------|
| Honest admission | "I've been wrong about [topic]." |
| Specific observation | "I've noticed something in every team that [outcome]." |
| Contrarian opinion | "Most [domain] advice optimizes for the wrong thing." |
| Personal story | "I [did X]. Best thing that ever happened." |
| Direct challenge | "[Common belief] is backwards. Here's why." |

**Retire these:** "I'm excited to announce...", "In today's rapidly evolving...", "This one thing made me $X", humblebrags.

## Formatting Rules

- One sentence per line (mobile readability)
- Blank line between paragraphs
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences max)
- Bold key phrases sparingly
- One emoji max, if any
- Hashtags at the very end after a line break (3-5, no inline)
- No external links in the post body (algorithm penalty — put links in first comment)

## Post Types

| Type | When to Use | Structure |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Story + lesson** | Personal experience relevant to ICP | Hook → context → tension → turning point → lesson → question |
| **List/tips** | Actionable advice from your domain | Hook → why it matters → numbered points (3-7) → closing insight |
| **Contrarian take** | Challenge conventional wisdom | Position → common belief → your reasoning → evidence → invite discussion |
| **Observation** | Industry pattern you've noticed | What you observed → evidence → why it matters → question |

## Algorithm Signals

| Signal | Impact | How |
|---|---|---|
| Dwell time | Very High | Longer posts people read fully |
| Comments | Very High | Ask genuine questions, create discussion |
| Saves | High | Actionable, reference-worthy content |
| "See more" clicks | High | Strong hook |
| External links | **Negative** | Put links in comments |
| Editing after posting | **Negative** | Don't edit within first hour |

## Content Pillars

Pick 3-5 topics you rotate through weekly. Every post should fit one pillar.

| Pillar | What It Covers |
|---|---|
| **Expertise** | Domain knowledge, how-tos from your ICP's world |
| **Stories** | Personal experiences, failures, wins |
| **Opinions** | Takes on industry trends, contrarian views |
| **Behind the scenes** | Building in public, process insights |
| **Curated insights** | Data, research summaries, trend analysis |

## Pre-Posting Checklist

- [ ] Would this stop MY scroll?
- [ ] Is there ONE clear idea?
- [ ] Does it sound like me, not "LinkedIn me"?
- [ ] Is it scannable with short paragraphs?
- [ ] Does it invite genuine engagement (not bait)?
- [ ] No external links in body (moved to comment)?
- [ ] Hook is under 210 characters?

## Output

Present the post with:
- **Pillar:** which content pillar this fits
- **Hook preview:** first 210 characters as the reader will see them
- **Full post:** formatted for LinkedIn
- **First comment:** link or additional context to post as first comment
- **Best time to post:** Tue-Thu, 7-8 AM or 12 PM in your audience's timezone
